Gold Fish

Goldfish don’t have stomachs Aug 25, 2022

Goldfish don’t have stomachs and should therefore be fed easily digestible food in lots of small feeding sessions, rather than lots of food at once. This is also why goldfish produce so much waste and why you need a filter to keep their water clean.

Crabs

Crabs don’t feel pain May 15, 2022

Their nervous systems aren’t complex enough to let them feel pain the way humans and other more evolved humans can. So there’s no real need to feel guilty when you’re tossing live crabs into a pot full of boiling water. How’s that for reassuring crab facts?

Sharks do not have bones

Sharks use their gills to filter oxygen from the water. They are a special type of fish known as "elasmobranchs", which translates into fish made of cartilaginous tissues—the clear gristly stuff that your ears and nose tip are made of. This category also includes rays, sawfish, and skates. Their cartilaginous skeletons are much lighter than true bone and their large livers are full of low-density oils, both helping them to be buoyant.
